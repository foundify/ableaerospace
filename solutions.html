Fixed-Wing Repairs

Always Advancing Our Fixed Wing Solutions

As an established leader in fixed wing component maintenance, Able is constantly perfecting its processes and surveying the market for new ways to serve aviation fleets across the globe. Whether you need support for Boeing, Douglas/Boeing or Airbus aircraft, count on Able’s leading-edge experience, all managed from within our 200,000-square-foot facility.

Through a staff of more than 30 engineers and DER experts, Able has developed 10,000+ FAA approved out of scope repairs, all with broad global airworthiness certifications. These jobs are completed 100 percent in house by our fixed-wing experts, allowing us to quickly and successfully tackle even your most complex issues – from evaluation to overhaul – and reduce your maintenance costs.

  • Engine Mounting Systems
  • Flap Tracks
  • Sequencing Carriages
  • Flap Transmissions
  • Stabilizer Trim Units
  • Landing Gear Components
  • Quadrants
